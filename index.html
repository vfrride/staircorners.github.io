Stair Corners

While wandering the back roads of the internet Steve and Jeff came upon something we'd never seen before... a strange triangular shape meant to add a little something extra to your house while serving an important purpose! These little triangles press into the corner of your stairs and keep dust bunnies from accumulating all the while adding a little bit of style that we rarely see in modern homes! Having some experience with working on old homes, with metal, and antiques we decided that it would be fun to come up with our own designs and offer customization services as well in case you want something that shows off your unique esthetic.
